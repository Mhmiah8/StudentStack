Frequently Asked Questions

What is StudentStack?

StudentStack is a student-focused dashboard for jobs, hackathons, notes, grants, and community collaboration in one place.

Do I need an account?

You can browse public opportunities without an account. An account is required to post teammate opportunities, upload notes, react, and comment.

How often is job data updated?

The scraper is configured to run daily and refreshes the latest jobs and related data files.

How can I report content?

Every note and teammate post includes a Report action. Moderators and admins review reported content.
